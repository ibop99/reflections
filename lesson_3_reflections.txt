Describe the differences between forks, clones, and branches.  When would you
use one instead of another?

    A fork makes a copy of a GitHub repository to your GitHub repository.
	A clone makes a copy of a GitHub repository onto your local machine or makes
	an additional copy on the local machine.
	A branch operates on your local copy and takes your history todate in a new direction.
